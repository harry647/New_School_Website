Welcome to Clubs & Societies at Bar Union Secondary

Clubs and societies are a core part of student life at Bar Union Secondary. They provide a platform for learners to discover talents, grow confidence, build leadership skills, and form meaningful connections beyond the classroom.

Why Join a Club?
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills
  • Explore interests in arts, science, technology, and service
  • Boost confidence and communication abilities
  • Improve academic and personal growth
What We Offer
  • 12 active clubs and societies
  • Student-led leadership opportunities
  • Inter-school competitions and events
  • Mentorship from dedicated teachers

Whether you are passionate about innovation, community service, creativity, or public speaking, there is a club where your ideas and talents matter.

Leadership Creativity Teamwork Innovation Community Impact

How to Join a Club or Society

Step 1

Login to your student account to access Clubs & Societies.

Step 2

Browse through available clubs and find one that matches your interest.

Step 3

Click Join and fill out the membership application form.

Step 4

Wait for approval and attend the first club meeting.
