Board of Management (BOM)

Providing Visionary Leadership & Strategic Oversight

The Board of Management (BOM) is the highest governing body at Bar Union Mixed Secondary School, entrusted with ensuring strategic direction, policy-making, and holistic management of the school. The BOM upholds the school's core values and guarantees compliance with the Education Act, while also promoting spiritual growth in alignment with the sponsoring African Inland Church.

Overview & Role

  • Oversees school policy, administration, and strategic vision
  • Chairperson (2017–2021): Mr. George Ominde, leading a competent and visionary team
  • Structured into committees handling Finance, Infrastructure, Discipline, and other key areas
  • Principal acts as Secretary, ensuring effective execution of BOM decisions
  • Collaborates closely with Parents' Association (PA) and the School Senior Management Committee (SMC)

Composition

  • Maintains gender balance with at least one-third female members
  • Representative Breakdown:
    • 3 members from the community
    • 4 members from the sponsoring church
    • 3 members from special interest groups
  • Total membership: Approximately 10–12, ensuring diversity, equity, and inclusivity

Supporting Committees

  • Parents' Association (PA)

    Executive arm of BOM, chaired by Mr. Charles Oswago. Includes 3 parent reps per class, 2 teacher reps, Principal & Deputy Principal.

  • School Senior Management Committee (SMC)

    Handles daily school operations, ensuring ministry directives and BOM policies are implemented. Members: Principal, Deputy Principal, Senior Master, HODs.

  • Student Leadership

    Prefects and club leaders link students with the BOM and SMC, ensuring adherence to rules and active participation in school programs.

Organizational Structure

  • BOM sits at the apex, setting vision, policies, and strategic priorities
  • Direct reports include: Principal, Deputy Principal, Director of Studies, and Bursar
  • Downstream structure: HODs, co-curricular coaches, support staff, Prefect Council, and club officials
  • Emphasizes top-down governance with integrated input from teachers, students, and parents

Leadership Team

Our leadership team is composed of experienced, dedicated, and visionary professionals committed to nurturing academic excellence, discipline, and strong moral values. Together, they guide the school toward continuous improvement and holistic student development.
