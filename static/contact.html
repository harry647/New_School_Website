Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about admissions, academics, alumni, and more

How can I schedule a school visit?

You can schedule a visit by filling out the contact form or calling our admissions office directly. Visits are guided and provide insight into classrooms, labs, and co-curricular facilities.

What are the admission deadlines?

Deadlines vary by grade. For Academic Year 2026–2027, registration opens November 1, 2025, and closes March 31, 2026. Entrance tests for higher grades are in early April. Please check the Admissions page for detailed timelines.

What documents are required for admission?

  • Birth Certificate / Age Proof
  • Previous School Report Card / Transcript
  • Passport-size Photo(s)
  • Identity documents of parent/guardian
  • Application form (online or printed)

Can I submit documents online?

Yes. Our online application form allows uploading scanned copies of required documents in PDF, DOC, JPG, or PNG formats.

Is there a dress code for students?

Yes. Students are expected to wear the prescribed uniform. Specific guidelines for formal, PE, and sports uniforms are provided upon admission.

Are there scholarships or fee concessions?

Yes. Scholarships are offered for academic excellence, sports, and co-curricular achievements. Sibling discounts and need-based concessions are also available. Details can be obtained from the admissions office.

How long does it take to get a reply after submitting the application?

Applications are reviewed promptly. We typically respond within 24–48 hours during working days with further instructions or interview schedules.

What curriculum does the school follow?

Bar Union Mixed Secondary School follows the national curriculum under the CBSE/State Board guidelines (adjust depending on school), emphasizing holistic development and examination readiness for national and international standards.

Are extra coaching or competitive exam preparations offered?

Yes. Our Career Guidance Cell provides preparation for competitive exams such as JEE, NEET, CLAT, CUET, and SAT, along with psychometric assessments and career counseling.

What co-curricular and extracurricular activities are available?

Students can participate in Robotics & AI, Drama & Theatre, Music & Dance, Art & Craft, sports teams, and club activities. We encourage participation for holistic development.

How does the school handle student counseling and career guidance?

Our dedicated Career Guidance & Counseling Cell helps students make informed decisions regarding streams, college applications, scholarships, internships, and career pathways. Sessions are personalized and free for students.

What safety measures are in place?

We prioritize student safety through security staff, CCTV surveillance, emergency drills, first aid facilities, and a well-defined discipline policy monitored by the BOM and staff.

Can alumni stay connected with the school?

Yes. Alumni can register through our Alumni Network form, participate in reunions, mentorship programs, and engage with current students.

Are parents involved in school decisions?

Parents participate through the Parents’ Association (PA), which collaborates with the BOM and SMC on policy implementation, events, and school programs.

How can I contact the school directly?

You can reach us via the contact form, email, phone, or visit during office hours. See our Contact Info & Map for full details.

What are the office hours?

Monday – Friday: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Sunday & Public Holidays: Closed

Where can I find fee details and important academic dates?

Fee structures and timelines are available in the Fees & Key Dates section and downloadable fee brochure.
