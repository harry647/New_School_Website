Senior School (Grades 10–12)

At Bar Union Senior School, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded students equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in life, higher education, and careers. Our Senior School program emphasizes academic excellence, practical skills, creativity, and moral development, ensuring each student discovers their potential and passion.

Our Core Focus

Every student, regardless of their chosen pathway, builds a strong foundation through four essential subjects:

  • English – mastering communication and expression
  • Kiswahili / Kenya Sign Language – promoting language skills and inclusivity
  • Community Service Learning – fostering empathy, responsibility, and leadership
  • Physical Education – developing fitness, teamwork, and perseverance

STEM Pathway

For students passionate about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, our STEM pathway offers diverse subjects to sharpen analytical thinking and innovation:

  • Mathematics – logical reasoning and problem solving
  • Physics – understanding the forces of nature
  • Chemistry – exploring matter and reactions
  • Biology – studying life and living systems
  • Computer Science – coding, software, and digital literacy
  • Aviation Technology – flight principles and aeronautics
  • Geospatial Technology – mapping, GIS, and earth observation
  • Agriculture – sustainable farming and food security
  • Home Science – practical life skills in health and nutrition
  • Drawing & Design – creativity and technical design skills
  • Building & Construction – practical engineering skills
  • Electrical Technology – power, circuits, and electronics
  • Metal Technology – fabrication and metalwork
  • Power Mechanics – mechanical systems and machinery
  • Wood Technology – carpentry and woodworking
  • Media Technology – communication, photography, and digital media
  • Marine & Fisheries Technology – aquatic ecosystems and fisheries management

Humanities & Social Sciences Pathway

Our Humanities pathway empowers students to understand society, culture, and human interaction while fostering critical thinking and leadership:

  • History & Citizenship – understanding our past and civic responsibilities
  • Geography – exploring the world and environmental systems
  • Religious Studies (CRE/IRE/HRE) – moral and spiritual development
  • Business Studies – entrepreneurship, management, and economics
  • Sociology & Philosophy – societal structures and ethical thinking
  • Languages – English, Kiswahili, French, German, Arabic, Mandarin
  • Literature in English – literary analysis and creative writing
  • Fasihi ya Kiswahili – Kiswahili literature and expression
  • Indigenous Languages – preserving cultural heritage

At Bar Union Senior School, we focus on holistic student development – academically, socially, and morally. Our pathways provide flexibility and choice, allowing each learner to excel in areas they are passionate about while building a strong foundation for future success.
