Transforming Learning with Technology

At Bar Union Mixed Secondary School, our E-Learning Portal empowers students, teachers, and parents with a seamless digital experience that complements classroom learning. Designed for flexibility, accessibility, and academic excellence, the portal ensures that learning never stops—whether at school or home.

  • Access interactive lessons, e-books, and resources 24/7
  • Submit assignments and receive timely feedback
  • Live classes & recorded sessions from expert teachers
  • Monitor performance with progress tracking and analytics
  • Collaborative forums for peer discussions and group projects
  • Rewards, quizzes, and gamified learning to motivate students
  • Compatible with mobile, tablet, and desktop devices

Our mission is to provide a **student-first digital learning environment** where knowledge, creativity, and critical thinking thrive. Teachers can enrich lessons with multimedia tools, parents can stay informed about academic progress, and students can explore subjects at their own pace.

Notifications & Alerts

Stay updated with automatic reminders for assignments, exams, live classes, school announcements, and upcoming events. Both students and parents receive timely alerts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries for students, parents, and teachers using the E-Learning portal

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email, and follow the instructions in the reset email to set a new password securely.

Can I access the portal from mobile devices?

Yes, the portal is fully responsive and accessible via mobile browsers. Additionally, dedicated apps for Android and iOS are available for enhanced experience and notifications.

How do I submit assignments and quizzes?

Log in to your class dashboard, locate the assignment or quiz, upload your file or complete online, and click "Submit". You will receive confirmation once submitted.

Can I view my submitted assignments?

Yes, all submissions are stored in your dashboard under "Assignments History" with timestamps and teacher feedback.

How can I track my performance?

Visit the "Performance Analytics" section to view detailed reports, grades, attendance, and progress trends for each subject and assessment.

Can parents track their child’s progress?

Yes, parents can log in using their credentials to monitor assignments, test scores, attendance, and teacher feedback in real-time.

Who do I contact for technical issues?

Use the "Technical Support" quick link in the portal or email support@your-school.edu. Our support team typically responds within a few hours on working days.

Why is my class video not loading?

Check your internet connection and browser compatibility. Clear cache or try a different browser. If issues persist, contact technical support with a screenshot.

Can I download study materials?

Yes, e-books, worksheets, and supplementary materials are available under the "Digital Study Material" section. Downloads are allowed as per copyright guidelines.

Are live classes recorded?

Yes, all live classes are recorded and uploaded in the portal. Students can access them anytime for revision.

How do I communicate with my teacher?

Use the "Doubt Clearing Forum" or messaging feature to ask questions. Teachers respond promptly to ensure clarity and support learning.

What should I do if I forgot my student ID?

Contact the school’s administration or support team to recover your student ID. Do not create multiple accounts.
