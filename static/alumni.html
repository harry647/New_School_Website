Bar Union Mixed Secondary School alumni continue to embody the values, knowledge, and life skills nurtured during their time at the school. Our graduates excel in diverse sectors, including academia, business, science, technology, arts, sports, and public service.

Through the structured alumni network, former students remain actively engaged by mentoring current learners, providing career guidance, supporting school initiatives, and contributing to infrastructure and community development projects. Membership in the alumni network also offers access to professional networking, collaborative opportunities, and exclusive events, reinforcing lifelong connections and a sense of belonging.
