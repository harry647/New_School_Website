Admission Process

Transparent, streamlined, and designed for parents and students

1. Inquiry & Online Registration

Start by filling our online inquiry form or download the admission form. Provide basic student information and preferred pathway (STEM, Humanities, or Sports & Arts).

2. School Visit & Orientation

Schedule a school tour to explore our classrooms, labs, sports facilities, and student life. Meet our admission counselors and the leadership team.

3. Entrance Assessment

Students undergo age-appropriate evaluation for placement in Senior School. Assessment includes core subjects and pathway-related optional subjects.

4. Parent Interaction & Guidance

Parents meet the Principal and admissions team for discussion on the student's academic pathway, co-curricular interests, and school expectations.

5. Admission Confirmation

Submit required documents, pay fees, and complete enrollment. Students are officially welcomed and ready to begin their learning journey.

Admission Requirements & Eligibility

Ensuring every student is prepared for a successful learning journey

Age & Grade Eligibility

Applicants must meet age requirements for Senior School. Transfer students must provide previous school records and KCPE/KCSE results.

Academic Records

Submission of official transcripts, school reports, and examination certificates required. Students expected to have passed core subjects.

Pathway Selection

Students choose a learning pathway and select optional subjects. Guidance provided to align with interests and career goals.

Health & Wellbeing

Submit recent medical report confirming fitness for school activities, including sports and laboratory work.

Identification Documents

Provide birth certificate, national ID (parents/guardians), and any legal documents for transfers or sponsorships.

Fees & Payments

All school fees must be paid through approved channels. Payments via unofficial methods may not be recognized.
