Admission Process

Transparent, streamlined, and designed for parents and students

1. Inquiry & Online Registration

Start by filling our online inquiry form or download the admission form. Provide basic student information and preferred pathway (STEM, Humanities, or Sports & Arts).

2. School Visit & Orientation

Schedule a school tour to explore our classrooms, labs, sports facilities, and student life. Meet our admission counselors and the leadership team to understand school culture and values.

3. Entrance Assessment

Students undergo age-appropriate evaluation for placement in Senior School (Grades 10–12). Assessment includes core subjects (English, Kiswahili, Community Service Learning, Physical Education) and pathway-related optional subjects (STEM, Humanities, Technical/Arts).

4. Parent Interaction & Guidance

Parents meet the Principal and admissions team for a comprehensive discussion on the student's academic pathway, co-curricular interests, and school expectations.

5. Admission Confirmation

Submit required documents, pay fees, and complete enrollment. Students are then officially welcomed into Bar Union Mixed Secondary School, ready to start their holistic learning journey.

Admission Requirements & Eligibility

Ensuring every student is prepared for a successful learning journey

Age & Grade Eligibility

Applicants must meet the age requirements for Senior School (Grades 10–12). Transfer students must provide previous school records and KCPE/KCSE results where applicable.

Academic Records

Submission of official academic transcripts, school reports, and examination certificates is required. Students are expected to have passed core subjects including English, Kiswahili, and Mathematics.

Pathway Selection

Students choose a learning pathway—STEM, Humanities, or Technical/Arts—and select optional subjects accordingly. Guidance is provided to ensure alignment with interests, strengths, and career goals.

Health & Wellbeing

Applicants should submit a recent medical report confirming fitness for school activities, including sports and practical laboratory work.

Identification Documents

Provide birth certificate, national ID (for parents/guardians), and any legal documents for transfers or sponsorships.

Fees & Payments

All school fees must be paid through the approved bank channels or our designated paybill number, as communicated via official letters and payment instructions.

Important: Payments made through unofficial channels or without following the provided instructions may not be recognized. Fee payment is required to secure the student's place in the chosen academic pathway.
